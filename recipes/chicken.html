Crispy Fried Chicken

Description:

Indulge in the classic comfort of our Crispy Fried Chicken. Each piece is marinated in a blend of buttermilk and spices, then coated in a seasoned flour mixture before being fried to golden perfection. This dish promises a satisfying crunch with every bite, making it a timeless favorite for any occasion.

Ingredients:

  • 1 (4 pound) chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour for coating
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 quarts vegetable oil for frying

Steps:

  1. Skin the chicken pieces if desired.
  2. Place flour in a plastic bag and season with paprika, salt, and pepper.
  3. Dip chicken in buttermilk, then coat in seasoned flour by shaking in the bag.
  4. Lay coated chicken on a tray and let it rest until the flour becomes paste-like.
  5. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet until very hot.
  6. Fry chicken in batches until browned on both sides.
  7. Reduce heat, cover, and cook for 30 minutes.
  8. Remove cover, increase heat, and fry until crispy.
  9. Drain on paper towels and keep warm in the oven while frying remaining batches.
